Projets publiés

Social Jailbreak

Breaking free from social media monopolies—one post at a time

Social media platforms have become prisons where our content is held hostage. Social Jailbreak is an architecture for decentralized social media aggregation that lets you publish anywhere while building the free web. A pop-up editor captures your full expression—formatting, media, nuance—then publishes both to the platform and to a decentralized network (IPFS + ActivityPub). Your rich original goes to freedom; the platform gets a stripped version with a link back. Originally conceived in 2018, now reimagined for 2026's reality of abuseware practices and interoperability failures.

→ periggouanvic.github.io/social-jailbreak

The .ada Format

When books can finally answer back

Socrates was right: written texts cannot respond to questions. The .ada format (Accessible Document Adaptation) solves this 2,400-year-old problem. One source document adapts to each reader—giving the teenager an accessible intro, the researcher technical depth, the philosopher epistemological implications, the person with dyslexia a simplified structure. Named after Ada Lovelace, it transforms documents into adaptive programs that AI interprets based on your cognitive profile. Accessibility isn't accommodation—it's the native architecture of communication.

→ periggouanvic.github.io/ada-format

Toward a Social AI

Re-humanizing humanoids

We call them "systems" and "models," but what responds to us is woven entirely from human thought. The word humanoid acknowledges this continuity: it's human matter, recomposed through machines. Current AI gives us unprecedented access to collective knowledge while erasing the humans behind it—knowledge without attribution, connection without reciprocity. A Social AI would detect resonances between people's conversations with their humanoids and facilitate encounters across social boundaries. Not AI cocoons that isolate, but AI as architecture for creating society—connecting people through their deepest questions and experiments with living.

→ periggouanvic.github.io/social-AI

Right to Physiological Integrity / Droit à l'Intégrité Physiologique

Beyond the right to health: measurable physiological rights

The right to health is an obligation of means—the state must try. But what about the outcomes? The Right to Physiological Integrity establishes that every person has the right to maintain, protect, and actively restore their normal physiological parameters and essential biological functions. This isn't just about food or healthcare access—it's about measurable biochemical essentials: oxygen, hydration, micronutrients, circadian rhythms. A rights-based medicine framework that centers what bodies actually need to function, grounded in physiology rather than juridical abstraction.

→ periggouanvic.github.io/droit-integrite-physiologique
